The Problem

Why kidneys, and why now?

Chronic Kidney Disease is the third fastest-growing cause of death globally, projected to become the 5th leading cause of life-years lost by 2040. The tragedy is that most damage happens silently, long before any symptoms appear.

"How might we integrate AI into modern medicine to detect kidney disease at its earliest stages to dramatically increase patient survival rates in the aging population?"

What is CKD?

Chronic Kidney Disease occurs when kidneys gradually lose their ability to filter waste from the blood. It is classified into 5 stages, and by the time symptoms appear, significant damage has already occurred.

FSGS: The Root Cause

Focal Segmental Glomerulosclerosis (FSGS) is a key driver of CKD. Scarring on the kidney's glomeruli causes vital proteins to leak into urine, a process that is silent but detectable through biomarkers early on.

The Aging Population

Kidney function naturally declines with age. 34% of adults over 65 have CKD. For those over 70, the compounding effects of hypertension, diabetes, and reduced GFR make early detection critical.

AI as the Solution

AI can detect patterns in biomarker data that humans cannot. By analysing blood pressure, glucose, and urinary protein levels continuously, machine learning models can predict CKD risk months before clinical diagnosis.

Current Treatment Gaps

Standard markers like serum creatinine only rise after 40–50% of kidney tissue is already damaged. Current care is reactive: patients are diagnosed late, treated late, and outcomes suffer as a result.

Our Approach

KidneyGuard shifts care from reactive to proactive. A wearable device continuously monitors key biomarkers and feeds data to an AI system that flags risk early, giving patients and doctors time to act.

The Device

KidneyGuard Hardware

A wearable patch worn on the wrist. Designed for simplicity with no setup, no configuration, and no technical knowledge required. Built specifically for people over 70 who may not own a smartphone or smartwatch.

System Interface
72 BPM
Active Monitoring
PPG · ECG · Bioimpedance

Engineered for absolute precision.

  • Continuous biomarker tracking

    Blood pressure, heart rate, glucose, and bioimpedance (a proxy for fluid retention and proteinuria) monitored around the clock.

  • Universal connectivity

    Syncs automatically to Apple Watch and Garmin via Bluetooth. For users without a smartwatch, a companion app on any smartphone handles everything.

  • On-device AI processing

    A lightweight AI model runs directly on the device, analysing trends in real time. Alerts are sent to the patient, carer, or GP when risk thresholds are crossed.

  • Extended Battery Optimization

    Designed for older users to charge once a week and wear it always. Water-resistant up to 50 meters.
